About Me

I am a first year astrophysics graduate student at the University of California, Berkeley. I completed dual degrees in physics and astrophysics with honors at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, working with Alexandra Pope on dusty galaxies for my undergraduate thesis on the TolTEC camera.

Outside of data wrangling, I play cello, as I have been a member in symphony orchestras continuously since I was in middle school. I love listening to and watching live classical music performances. I also love to travel to national parks and historic sites around the world.
