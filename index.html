About Me

I am a first year astrophysics graduate student at the University of California, Berkeley. I completed dual degrees in physics and astrophysics with a mathematics minor at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, working with Alexandra Pope on dusty galaxies for my undergraduate thesis on the TolTEC camera.

Outside of data wrangling, I play cello and piano. Having been a member in youth symphony orchestras continuously since I was in middle school, I love listening to and watching live classical music performances. I also especially love to travel to national parks and historic sites around the world, of which I keep track.
